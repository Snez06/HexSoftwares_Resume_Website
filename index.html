Our Programs

Targeted initiatives making real impact.

Emergency Rescue

Rapid field response, transport, and triage for animals in crisis, coordinated with local authorities.

Rehabilitation Centre

On-site veterinary care, nutrition plans, and enrichment activities to help animals recover fully.

Adoption & Foster

Screening, matching, and support for adopters; foster network for animals needing extra care.

Community Vet Clinics

Weekly mobile clinics offering vaccinations, microchipping, deworming, and basic checkups.

Seasonal drives in low-income areas to reduce overpopulation humanely and sustainably.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are my donations tax-deductible?

Yes. We are a registered public benefit organization and provide Section 18A tax receipts for qualifying donations.

How are funds used?

90% goes directly to programs (rescue, vet care, adoptions, education). 10% covers essential admin and fundraising costs.

Can I visit the shelter?

Absolutely! Visits are by appointment to ensure animal wellbeing. Contact us to schedule.

Do you accept international donations?

Yes. We accept international card payments and bank transfers in multiple currencies.
